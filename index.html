What is Rickards Key Club?

Key Club is a student-led service organization that focuses on helping others and improving the community through volunteer work. It is part of Key Club International and encourages members to develop leadership skills, build friendships, and make a positive impact. Through activities like community cleanups, fundraising events, and supporting local causes, Key Club members learn the importance of teamwork, responsibility, and giving back.

Why Rickards Key Club?

Build Leadership Skills

Key Club helps students grow as leaders by giving them opportunities to plan events, take on officer roles, and work as a team. Members gain confidence and real-world skills that are useful in school and beyond..

Make a Difference in the Community

Through volunteer projects and service events, Key Club members directly help their community. Whether it’s organizing fundraisers or supporting local causes, students see the impact of their efforts.

Meet New People and Have Fun

Key Club is a great way to make friends who share similar values. Members bond through service projects, meetings, and events, creating lasting friendships while enjoying meaningful activities.
